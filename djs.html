Artists & energy

DJs

DJs shape electronic music culture through sound, identity, and performance. This page introduces artists connected to gabber, hardcore, techno, and drum & bass.

Angerfist

Gabber / Hardcore

Angerfist is one of the most iconic names in hardcore and gabber, known for aggressive high-BPM tracks, dark energy, and a strong visual identity.

Miss K8

Hardcore / Industrial

Miss K8 is a major figure in contemporary hardcore, combining dark atmosphere, festival intensity, and industrial energy.

Amelie Lens

Techno

Amelie Lens is known for fast, hypnotic techno sets with strong momentum, driving rhythms, and global festival presence.

Charlotte de Witte

Techno / Industrial

Charlotte de Witte’s sound is minimal, dark, and precise, with a strong underground character and industrial atmosphere.

Andy C

Drum & Bass

Andy C is a legendary drum & bass DJ known for technical mixing, speed, and high-energy sets that helped define the genre.

Chase & Status

Drum & Bass / Bass Music

Chase & Status blend drum & bass with broader bass music styles, creating a powerful, cinematic sound with strong festival energy.
