DJs

DJs shape electronic music culture through performance, atmosphere, and energy. This page highlights a few artists associated with different scenes and sounds.

Amelie Lens

Techno / Dark / Driving

Amelie Lens is a Belgian DJ and producer known for fast, intense techno sets. Her style is dark, energetic, and highly immersive, making her one of the most recognizable figures in contemporary European club culture.

Charlotte de Witte

Techno / Hypnotic / Industrial

Charlotte de Witte is known for stripped-back, hypnotic techno with a strong industrial edge. Her sets often build tension through repetition, pressure, and a cold, precise atmosphere.

Indira Paganotto

Psy-Techno / Trance / Festival

Indira Paganotto blends techno with psychedelic and trance influences, creating fast-paced sets that feel emotional, cosmic, and festival-driven. Her sound connects club intensity with a more spiritual rave atmosphere.
